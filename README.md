# Project Background

The pizza sales company sells many different styles of pizza (VEGGIE | CHICKEN | SUPREME | CLASSIC) and our task is to analyze the data given to us in order to report back an overview of our findings. The timeline will consist of one full year of data from 1/1/2015 - 12/12/2015. 

# KPI's tracked:
* Total revenue
* Average order value
* Total pizzas sold
* Total orders
* Average pizza per order

# 1st dashboard will include:
* Hourly trend for total pizzas sold
* Weekly trend for total orders
* Percentage of sales by pizza category
* Percentage of sales by pizza size,
* Total orders & pizzas sold by pizza category

# 2nd dashboard will include:
* Top 5 & bottom 5 pizzas by revenue
* Top 5 & bottom 5 pizzas by total sold
* Top 5 & bottom 5 pizzas by total orders
